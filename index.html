Best Person

Awarded to the Nos Amian with the greatest and/or funniest contributions overall to the server as well as the world. Sadly, since no one has solved a Millennium Problem or gotten a Pulitzer Prize yet, we'll still have to vote.





Best Gamer

Awarded to the Nos Amian with the greatest skills in the art of video-gaming or the most active contributions to the server in such fields. Success in this category may or may not directly correlates with the quality of one's personal computer.





Best Woman

Awarded to one of the two known women. Nos Amian scientists are still hard at work, in the hopes of eventually discovering a third woman and expanding this category.


Best Artist

Awarded to the Nos Amian with the greatest artistic skills or the most active contributions in the artistic fields (literature, visual arts, music etc.). You could say that winning this is an art in and of itself.





Best New Or Returning Nos Amian

Awarded to the best new or returning face among Nos Amian kind. Whether as a traveller returning from long-time exile or as a brand-new and well-vetted new entrance to the community, every nominee here has added value to the Nos Amis experience.




Best Death

Awarded to the Nos Amian (or not) who died in the most spectacular and/or interesting and/or funny fashion. For these nominees, death is temporary, but class is eternal.





Best Non-Nos Amian Non-Foreign Person

Awarded to the non-Nos Amian person (presumably) of Vietnamese descent with the greatest and/or funniest contributions overall to the server. Why this category and the one below it are separate are still unknown.





Best Non-Nos Amian Foreign Person

Awarded to the non-Nos Amian person (presumably) not of Vietnamese descent with the greatest and/or funniest contributions overall to the server. Why this category and the one above it are separate are still unknown.





Best Führer

Awarded to the Nos Amian under whose leadership the server reached its greatest potential. All hail my benevolence for putting this category in in the first place and not just proclaiming myself as the winner!




Best Bot

Awarded to the machine-type Nos Amian with the most positive impact on the community. Ignoring the fact that one of the five options is not even a real bot, the nominees are all either helpful to Nos Amians or incredibly funny.

Best Game

Awarded to the most riveting and/or interesting and/or funny video game that was part of the Nos Amian discourse this past year. Note that being a good game is not a prerequisite for this award.





Best Non-Spamtung-Type Anime

Awarded to the most riveting and/or interesting and/or funny anime that was part of the Nos Amian discourse this past year. These animes came from all spectrums of the animated world, and gave us a wide variety of emotions, from boredom to dread to the fabled hygge.





Best Spamtung-Type Anime

Awarded to the most tolerable generic show/bad romcom/straight up fetish-fueled nightmare that was part of the Nos Amian discourse this past year. Not a best-of list by any means.





Best Emoji

Awarded to the best emoji created and utilized by Nos Amians in their daily conversations. A certain someone may or may not have forgotten to add :what: to preliminary voting, so please have five seconds of silence for what could have been a finalist before voting.





Best Event

Awarded to the most influential/grandest/funniest event held and enjoyed by Nos Amians. As occasions that we all look forward to, these events united us all for the finest moments of the community.





Best Made-Up Word

Awarded to the most meaningful and/or humorous new slang created by Nos Amians. To be absolutely frank, by "Nos Amians", we mean Jord.





Best Trip

Awarded to the most tolerable and/or interesting physical adventures that involve some or most Nos Amians. Some might say that memories on the road are those most treasured, but for some of these nominees, that might not be the case.





Best Roblox Game

Awarded to the most interesting and/or funny game on the Roblox platform that was part of the Nos Amis discourse this past year. Until Jord finally gets his head out of his ass and learns Lua, we'll have to be content with naming others' games in this category.
